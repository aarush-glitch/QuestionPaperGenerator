Case Overview E ABC is a new pharmaceutical organization starting its sales operations in this month. They will be launching a new drug Domeperidone capsules for nausea and vomiting. Since this is a new drug, they do not anticipate much sales for this product in the launch quarter. Hence, they have kept minimum sales force who will go to physicians/outlets to sell their drugs. The management team wants to analyze if the sales force structure is appropriate calculate their incentive compensation. The case study is about calculating metrics and helping business analyze their data in a smart way to better forecast and plan for their future development.
